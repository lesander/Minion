      Welcome to Minion!

      Minion is an application that connects with your Popcorn Time client running on your pc. With Minion, you can control and stream Popcorn Time's videos right to any of your devices!

      To get started, fill in the connection details of your Popcorn Time client. Need some help? We've written an article just for you, see here.
